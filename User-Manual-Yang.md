User Manual for Yang

My role
- I am working on cybersecurity analyst tasks.
- The days and times I work are:
  - Monday to Friday 12 pm to 2 pm
  - Additional times or changes may occur depending on circumstances.

How I Work Best
- I do my best work when:
  - I am given clear instructions or requirements with soft deadlines.
  - I am given some freedom to decide on my degree of focus.
- I struggle when:
  - I am not given enough structure regarding tasks and times.
  - I am not in the right headspace for working.
  - I am anticipating a scheduled appointment.

Communication Style
- I prefer messages to be...
  - Short and direct if verbal.
  - Detailed if visual or async.
- The best way to reach me is...
  - Email, discord messages or text.
- If you need something urgent, do this...
  - Text followed by a phone call.

Collaboration & Teamwork
- I work best in a team when...
  - I understand the purpose of the team and my role in it.
  - I have time to familiarise myself with my team members.
- My biggest pet peeve in teamwork is...
  - Lack of communication or clarity.
  - Excessive meetings.
- If I seem quiet in a meeting, it usually means...
  - I'm thinking about the subject and considering my words.
  - I'm having connection issues.

Focus & Productivity
- I stay focused by...
  - Staying mentally occupied.
- Things that distract me...
  - My surroundings.
  - Boredom.
- The best way to support my productivity is...
  - Provide clear instructions/requirements and deadlines.
  - Give me one task at a time or an ordered list of tasks.

Feedback & Growth
- I like feedback to be...
  - Direct, gentle, with explanation or examples.
  - Private
- When I make a mistake, the best way to let me know is...
  - Messages
- I appreciate it when teammates...
  - Discuss over problems and solutions together.

Fun Facts & Quirks
- One thing people often misunderstand about me is...
  - I may appear detached only because I had no reason to act otherwise.
- A fun fact about me...
  - Ask in person.
- My work superpower is...
  - The closer the deadline the faster I work.
